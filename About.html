About Me



My name is Christy Inthoulay and I am a student currently attending high-school. I created this website because I wanted to share my art to others, since it is a way I express myself.

As an Asian-American I also would like to share my life struggles through art to show hardships of being Asian in America. Another reason I made this website is to share my culture because I am Laotian and many don't even know that Laos exists!

It is my first time creating a website, but I hoped you enjoyed your stay at Chrisy's Cafe!









Q&A

Do you want to become a professional artist in the future?

I would have to be really skilled to be considered a professional artist and it would take a lot of work to get there. At the moment, I think that art will stay as a hobby because I'm not skilled enought to have art as a career.


Do you like any games?

My favorite games are Roblox and Minecraft! (Roblox is technically a website but it's a website with games on it so idc :p)


Do you enjoy coding?

I think coding is pretty cool but it is a lot of work when you're new to it and can be confusing at times.


How long have you been drawing?

I've been drawing since I was a little kid, I remember it all started as stick figures and then I eventually developed my own art style. I started to take art seriously during middle school, but I lost motivation and stopped drawing for two years. I got back into art in highschool because I got bored during quarantine....


Do you like coffee?

The whole theme of this website is a cafe, so you would assume that the creator would like coffee, but I actually hate coffee. The only coffee I would accept is a sweet iced coffee, but that's it! I remember when I was younger I took a sip of my dad's coffee and it was really bitter, which was disgusting.
